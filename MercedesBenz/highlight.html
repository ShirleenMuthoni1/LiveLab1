The following are the highlights of Mercedes-Benz over the years:

  1. Introduction of the first production car in 1901 by Mercedes Benz founder Carl Benz.
  2. Invention of the first antilock braking system in 1978.
  3. Launching of the first airbag system in 1981.
  4. Introduction of the Electronic Stability Program in 1995.
  5. Developmemnt of the first hybrid car, the S400 Hybrid in 2009.
  6. Launch of the autonomous driving technology in the Mercedes Benz S-Class in 2013.
  7. Introduction of the EQ brand for electrical cars in 2016.
  8. Unveiling of the vision urbanetic concept in 2018, showcasing future mobility solutions.
  9. Collaboration with NVIDIA for AI driven vehicles in 2020.
  10. Launch of the fully electric EQS luxury sedan in 2021.
